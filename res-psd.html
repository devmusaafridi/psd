Proudly serving as your local family funeral directors since 1882

Joseph Edwards & Sons have been arranging and conducting funerals in the local community for over 130 years. The family owned business in in the hands of the fourth generation of the Edwards family that started in 1882.

The independent funeral profession has a long and proud history of personal service to local communities, carrying on the family tradition is a way of life for Joseph Edwards & Sons, who are deeply committed to upholding professional standards.
